üëë Community Roles (How to Earn)

These are the badges of honor within the Ritualnet ecosystem, often earned through community interaction and participation:

  • @Blessed - Earned by receiving **5 Blessings** from other members. Your initial step into community recognition.
  • @Cursed - Earned by receiving **5 Curses** from other members. Embracing the duality of the Ritual.
  • @Harmonic - Earned by achieving **5 Blessings and 5 Curses**. This role signifies balance and active engagement in the community's magic.
  • @Ritualist Ascendant - The highest honor, earned by reaching **Level 10**, alongside **10 Blessings** and **10 Curses**.
  • @Initiate - You're new to the community and have made it through the verification process. **Welcome!**
  • @Ritualist - Becoming a Ritualist is a high honor, meaning you have authentically demonstrated your commitment to our project by consistently contributing value (outside of Bless/Curse metrics).
  • @Mage - You are a Ritualist with a mage specialization. Mages create written content and conjure art or memes that grow the community.
  • @Forerunner - Forerunners come from the time before Ritual. A limited number made it through the void. If you did, you're early.
  • @Node Runner - You've deployed a Node on Ritual Infernet. Earn this role by being verified on our guild page.
